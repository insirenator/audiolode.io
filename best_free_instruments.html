Best Free Instrument Plugins

Keyzone Classic Piano

Keyzone Classic is the most realistic sounding free sample based piano plugin. Created by Bitsonic, the plugin features great sounding presets like Steinway Piano, Yamaha Grand Piano, Rhodes, etc. It also provides Envelope control, an LFO and a built in reverb. It's my favorite piano plugin to use in my own music tracks. It delivers an excellent sound quality. You can download it for free and try it out.

Get Keyzone

Ample Guitar M Lite II

Ample Guitar M Lite II is a very realistic sounding sampled acoustic guitar plugin created by Ample Sounds. Ample Sound is known for making a wide variety of paid high quality guitar and bass plugins. This one, they gave out for free and trust me, it's pretty amazing for a free plugin. Loaded with articulations like Sustain, Hammer On & Pull Off, Palm Mute and Popping, it also features many effect pedals like reverb, delay, chorus, flanger and compressor. Definitely check this one out.

Get Ample Guitar

Ample Bass P Lite II

Also created by Ample Sounds and given up for free, Ample Bass P Lite II is a bass plugin recorded on a Fender Precision Bass. It's my favorite bass plugin because it has a very unique sound which you can't get from any other free plugin. I always use this bass in my tracks. It's a must have!

Get Ample Bass

French Violin

This violin plugin is developed by Sample Science, and they describe it as,"A beautiful multi-sampled violin with a lot of character and charm." If you ask me, I think this plugin has the most pleasing and beautiful sound among all the other free violin plugins. And more importantly, it's light-weight and doesn't consume much of your CPU. Loaded with 6 different articulations, a room reverb and much more, this plugin is a must have in your collection.

Get French Violin

EWHarp2

Developed by Versilian Studions, EtherealWinds Harp II CE is a stereo sampled 34- string Iona Celtic Harp. This plugin has a very beautiful and high quality sound considering the fact that it's totally free of cost. If you want to add some ethereal vibe and ethnic quality to your music tracks, definitely try this one out.

Get EWHarp2
